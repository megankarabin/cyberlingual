about

Hi, I'm Megan. I'm a full-time Master's student in cognitive science of language at McMaster University, and I program and do data science in my spare time. Python is my mother tongue, but I've been using R more recently for academic work (expect to see some posts about R soon).

Other than English, I speak French and German, I think I can say I've mastered the basics of Portuguese (it's a work in progress), and I most recently started learning Russian. Ultra beginner at the moment...I'll be back with an update in 6 months. In my free time I play piano, do yoga, read philosophy and hang out with my dog. And pinball. I really love pinball.
